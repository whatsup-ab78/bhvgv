"About Us"

The All India Association of Sports For All [(AIASFA) is the Honorary Association, affiliated with The Association for International Sport for All (TAFISA) and Asiana Sport for All (ASFAA) as the umbrella organization and electoral right body on behalf of India since 2001 onwards. The All India Association of Sports For All [(AIASFA) Association, Registered under the Societies Act XXI 1860] since 2001 at Delhi. Its' aim is to keep alive Traditional Games, Prime Sports developments and better physical activities at all level in the country.
